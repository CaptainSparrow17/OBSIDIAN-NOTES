[Intelligent Platform Management Interface](https://www.thomas-krenn.com/en/wiki/IPMI_Basics) (`IPMI`)
IPMI (Intelligent Platform Management Interface) is a standardized, hardware-level interface that allows administrators to remotely monitor, manage, and troubleshoot servers independently of the host's CPU, firmware, or operating system. By communicating directly with the motherboard's Baseboard Management Controller (BMC), IPMI operates even if the machine is powered off or the OS crashes.
